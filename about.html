the origins of brat

The "brat Summer" trend began after the release of singer Charli xcx's album, titled "brat". The styling of the album cover, along with the successful marketing of the album itself, led to worldwide success for the singer, who had previous hits with Icona Pop ("I Love It") as well as Iggy Azealia ("Fancy") and as an individual artist with "Boom Clap", which featured on the sountrack to The Fault In Our Stars (2014).

But ten years later, Charli xcx has a bigger fan base than ever.
